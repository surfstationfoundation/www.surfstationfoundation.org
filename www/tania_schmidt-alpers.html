Tania Schmidt-Alpers

Tania is a bar licensed attorney in Connecticut (1995), District of Columbia (1996) and Florida (1997). In 2016 and again in 2017, Tania was recognized by the American Institute of Family Law Attorneys as one of the "10 Best Attorneys" for Florida. Tania has been active in systemic advocacy through Pro Bono appellate cases resulting in the creation of new case law for survivors of domestic, sexual and dating violence throughout the State of Florida. This is truly her passion and life's work.
