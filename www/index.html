Bringing the Joy of Surfing to the Community

The Surf Station Foundation is a nonprofit organization with a mission to TODO.

 

This Saturday, August 27, the Nation's Oldest City will host an event with Surfers Healing at the south side of the St. Augustine Beach pier from 10am-4pm. Surfers Healing is in organization that takes kids with autism surfing, giving over 4,500 surfers and their families a day of fun in and sun in just one year. Throughout this event, our community will give some great kids the opportunity to experience the pure joy that is surfing. The St. Augustine Boardrider's Club will be on the scene, volunteering their time and skills. If you would like to be a part of this awesome event, you can get some more information here.
