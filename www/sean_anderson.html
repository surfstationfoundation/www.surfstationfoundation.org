Sean Anderson

Sean Anderson has a lifetime of surfing experience including surfing professionally on the PSSA "Bud Tour", the ASP East, and the ASP world tour (now known as the WSL). He has over 25 years of coaching and teaching surfing to his credit including working with celebrities including Bruce Springsteen, Adam Sandler, Cameron Diaz, Billy Joe Armstrong (of Green Day) to mention just a few. He is the creator of the "Level Up" system of surfing instruction and is a founding member and owner of the Ohana Surf Project in Honolulu, Hawaii.
